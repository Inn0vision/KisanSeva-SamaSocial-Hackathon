# Kandi Land
Kandi is a local term from the Kashmir region of India for semi-barren, rainfed farmland. This soil type is characterized by its low fertility and reliance on rainfall. It has been successfully used for cultivating resilient crops like lavender, which require minimal inputs and can thrive in such conditions.
