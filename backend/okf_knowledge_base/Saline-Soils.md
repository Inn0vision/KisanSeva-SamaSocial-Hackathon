# Saline Soils
Saline soils are mentioned in the context of the Kuttanad below-sea-level farming system in India. This system has allowed for the development of specific genetic resources (e.g., rice varieties) that can grow in saline conditions. The conservation of these adapted genetic resources on-farm, along with the knowledge to manage them, represents a local response to community needs for coping with climate change impacts.
