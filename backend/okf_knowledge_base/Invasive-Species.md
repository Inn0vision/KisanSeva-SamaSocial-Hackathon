# Invasive Species
Invasive species are aggressive, over-abundant species that can pose a threat to biodiversity and ecosystem functioning. Controlling them is a process-oriented management strategy for protecting biodiversity and resilience in commodity production landscapes. These species can act as [[Pests]] in agricultural contexts.
