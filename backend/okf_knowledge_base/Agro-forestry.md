# Agro-forestry
A land use management system that combines trees and shrubs with crops and/or livestock. It is listed as an example of an activity that could receive financing under the GEF-6 strategy for Climate-Smart Agriculture. It is also a practice for which carbon accounting methodologies have been developed under voluntary carbon market standards.
