# Recycle aquaculture systems
Fresh or saltwater systems in tanks or ponds with substantial water treatment and reuse; primarily used for hatcheries or high-value species. These systems aim for maximum recycle rates to maximize independence from the external environment. Responses to climate risks include system operating adjustments, with open systems potentially using more recycle to stabilize water quality. Higher external variability may require greater system control, backup provision, and high recycle rates, with tradeoffs with energy costs becoming more important.
