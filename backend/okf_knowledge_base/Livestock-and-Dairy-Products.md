# Livestock and Dairy Products
Refers to products derived from animals, including meat and milk. Losses and waste occur due to poor feeding, diseases (e.g., swine fevers, tick-borne, zoonotic diseases), poor milking techniques, and inadequate handling during transport and storage. Good practices include proper animal management, nutrition, milking hygiene, and vaccinations.
