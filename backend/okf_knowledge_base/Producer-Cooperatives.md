# Producer Cooperatives
Producer cooperatives and unions are formal institutions intended to reflect producers' interests. They provide or coordinate demand-driven extension services to their members and represent stakeholder interests on a political level. They are crucial for building a broad base of support for new practices like Climate-Smart Agriculture (CSA) but can be limited in capacity and are sometimes open to manipulation by governments and elites. They are key partners for promoting CSA adoption.
