# Manure Management
Manure management refers to the proper handling and utilization of animal waste. It is essential for maintaining soil fertility, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and promoting sustainable agriculture practices.

# Manure Management
An agricultural practice focused on the handling of animal waste. It is an established niche for methane avoidance and is cited as one of the specific niches (alongside product certification) that underdeveloped financing channels like carbon markets might support in the mid-term. It represents a viable area for agricultural GHG emission reductions.
