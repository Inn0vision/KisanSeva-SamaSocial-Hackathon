# Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO)
A United Nations agency that leads international efforts to defeat hunger. It provides a Capacity Assessment tool for institutional analysis and published a training guide on gender and climate change with CCAFS. FAO also launched the Community for Climate Change Mitigation in Agriculture, an online network for practitioners working on reducing agricultural greenhouse gas emissions.
