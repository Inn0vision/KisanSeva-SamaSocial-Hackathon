# Sand Encroachment
Sand encroachment is a process of land degradation where sand spreads over productive land, often associated with desertification. In the Syrian Badia, reintroducing native plants in rangeland management projects aims to "stop sand encroachment," alongside [[Soil Fixation|fixing the soil]], as part of ecosystem restoration efforts for [[Rangelands]].
