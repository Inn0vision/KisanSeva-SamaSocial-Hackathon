# Cotton
Cotton is identified as one of the crops whose areas at high climatic risk are projected to increase due to climate change, specifically due to increased evapotranspiration.

# Cotton
Cotton is a type of fiber crop. Its global production status is noted as variable, with declining production in some regions and growth in others, contributing to the overall "Fiber" sub-category of provisioning services.
