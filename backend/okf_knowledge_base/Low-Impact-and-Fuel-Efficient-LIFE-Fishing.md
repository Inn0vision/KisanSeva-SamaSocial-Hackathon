# Low Impact and Fuel Efficient (LIFE) Fishing
A fishing approach characterized by low cost and ease of use of gear, which maintains its preferred usage. It may become more significant as a response to major climate disruptions. The method is considered a strategic shift to reduce the carbon footprint of capture fisheries, where fuel use is a primary source of GHG emissions.
