# Alpine Meadow
Alpine meadows, such as those on the eastern Tibetan Plateau, are ecosystems where soil carbon and nitrogen levels are influenced by factors like grazing intensity. Research has focused on methane emissions from alpine wetlands in the Zoige Plateau, which are often associated with alpine meadow environments.
