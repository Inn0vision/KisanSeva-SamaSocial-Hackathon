# Global Environment Facility (GEF)
Serves as an operating entity of the financial mechanism of the UNFCCC. Through the GEF Trust Fund, it provides financing to cover the incremental costs developing countries incur for activities that create global environmental benefits, including climate change mitigation. It also manages the Least Developed Countries Fund (LDCF) and the Special Climate Change Fund (SCCF) for adaptation. GEF funding is combinable, meaning a single project can access funding from different GEF channels (e.g., combining mitigation and adaptation funds), which is highly suitable for integrated CSA activities. Its strategies dictate funding priorities.
