# Longlines
A type of fishing gear used in both small-scale and commercial fishing. In small-scale contexts, it is a passive gear. In commercial fishing, it is used for key pelagic and other fisheries, often on the open seas with international scope. Climate change impacts include changes in distribution across wider zonal areas and increased effort per catch. Response strategies for commercial operations include extending effort, improving stock monitoring and location options, and moving to higher value markets.
