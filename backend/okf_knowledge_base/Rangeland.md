# Rangeland
Rangelands are areas used for grazing livestock. In pastoral areas, rangelands are susceptible to impacts from climate change, such as drying water points and variability in animal feed. Rangeland privatization, as seen in the Zoige wetlands on the Eastern Tibetan Plateau, can have significant impacts on these ecosystems.
