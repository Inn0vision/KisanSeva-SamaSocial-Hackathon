# Poor Quality Soil
Soils that may lack essential nutrients, have unfavorable structure, or other properties that limit optimal plant growth. Some animal breeds, developed through centuries of natural selection and domestication, possess the capacity to utilize scarce and poor quality feed derived from such soils, demonstrating valuable genetic adaptations to challenging environmental conditions.
