# Acidic Soils
Acidic soils have a pH level between 4 and 7. In these soils, acidity reduces bacterial activity, which consequently impairs the decomposition of [[Soil Organic Matter (SOM)]] and the release of [[Soil Nutrients]]. Nitrogen-fixing [[Rhizobia]] and [[Legumes]] generally do not thrive in acidic conditions, limiting their contribution to soil fertility.
