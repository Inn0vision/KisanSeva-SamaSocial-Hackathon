# Silty Soils
Silty soils have a higher proportion of silt particles, giving them properties intermediate between [[Sandy Soils]] and [[Clay Soils]]. They possess a greater capacity to retain moisture and nutrients through chemical attraction compared to sandy soils, contributing to higher productivity.
