# Crops Adapted to High Soil Moisture
These are crops specifically chosen for cultivation in conditions of high soil moisture, particularly relevant for managing [[Peatlands]] where rewetting is not feasible. Selecting such crops helps minimize drainage, reduce [[Peat Oxidation]], and limit [[Land Degradation]]. This approach is part of sustainable agricultural production on [[Peatlands]].
