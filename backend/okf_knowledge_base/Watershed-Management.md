# Watershed Management
The process of creating and implementing plans, programs, and projects to sustain and enhance watershed functions that affect the plant, animal, and human communities within a watershed boundary. The PSNP in Ethiopia uses a Community-Based Participatory Watershed Development approach, which is a sophisticated and integrated method for restoring degraded environments and building resilience at a landscape level, a key aspect of implementing CSA.
