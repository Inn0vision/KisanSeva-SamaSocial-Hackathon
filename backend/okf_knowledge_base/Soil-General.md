# Soil (General)
A fundamental component of agro-ecosystems, crucial for crop production and overall environmental health. Healthy soil enhances soil-related ecosystem services and crop nutrition. Conversely, [[Degraded Soils]], [[Soil Erosion]], and [[Waterlogging of Soils]] are major challenges that reduce yields and arable land.

Soil management practices are vital for sustainable crop production, climate change adaptation, and mitigation. These include maintaining healthy soil to improve structure and water storage capacity, which helps plants survive drought and reduces susceptibility to flooding and erosion. Increased levels of [[Organic Matter]] in soil contribute to climate change mitigation by storing carbon from atmospheric CO2 (carbon sequestration) and reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Practices like [[Conservation Agriculture]] and [[Integrated Nutrient and Soil Management]] are key to achieving these benefits.
