# Agricultural Pests
Agricultural pests are identified as a significant risk to agricultural production, alongside [[Agricultural Diseases]]. Climate change is expected to affect the nature, extent, and intensity of these risks, including "plant pests." The text emphasizes the need to cope with increased incidence of pests due to changing climate conditions and the importance of preserving genetic resources to develop pest-resistant varieties.
