# Gill nets
A type of passive fishing gear used in small-scale fishing, which is a significant global sector mainly in coastal areas. It is a major source of market and domestic supply with low capital and energy needs. This gear is associated with fishing systems that may experience shifts in species mix, timing, and locations due to climate change. Response strategies include adapting gear, timing, and access, and possibly developing advantages through greater research and development.
