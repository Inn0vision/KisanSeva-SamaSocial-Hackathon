# Manure
Manure is animal excreta used as a natural fertilizer and soil conditioner. It can replace synthetic fertilizers, contributing to energy efficiency and sustainable income. Manure is also a key feedstock for biogas production in biodigesters, with the by-product (slurry/liquid fertilizer) being returned to crops to increase yields and improve soil quality and organic matter.
