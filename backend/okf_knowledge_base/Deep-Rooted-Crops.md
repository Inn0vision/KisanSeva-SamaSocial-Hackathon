# Deep Rooted Crops
Crops characterized by extensive root systems that penetrate deep into the soil profile. The text mentions "breeding deep rooted crops" as a soil management option to increase soil carbon stocks and notes that deep rooting species "can cycle nutrients and water from deep in the soil profile."
