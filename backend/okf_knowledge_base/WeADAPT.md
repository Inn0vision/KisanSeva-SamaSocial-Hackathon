# WeADAPT
An online 'open space' on climate change adaptation issues with over 1,500 members from more than 200 organizations. Members share case studies, tools, and advice in thematic 'hubs' to build a professional community of practice. It provides services like automatically connecting members with similar interests and providing access to international climate data through collaboration with the Climate Information Portal of the University of Cape Town.
