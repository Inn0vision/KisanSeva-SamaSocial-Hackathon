# MILKPRO
A low-cost, simple milk pasteurizing and packaging unit. It fills raw milk into pre-formed polyethylene sachets, which are then sealed and pasteurized within the sachet at 65 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes. This process largely avoids post-pasteurization contamination, giving the milk a refrigerated shelf-life of up to 15 days. It is designed for small-scale processors in developing countries.
