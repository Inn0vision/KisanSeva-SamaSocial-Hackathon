# Completely fed inland/coastal pond systems
A gradually intensified or purpose-planned aquaculture system for highly stocked fish or shrimp. It features high water exchange, aeration, and treatment. This system is vulnerable to temperature, salinity, and other water quality instabilities. Response strategies include better system management, backup systems, adjusting species, and possibly intensifying or reducing crop cycles. For major disruptions, possible redesign, reconstruction, or relocations are considered, along with expansion into salinized land areas.
