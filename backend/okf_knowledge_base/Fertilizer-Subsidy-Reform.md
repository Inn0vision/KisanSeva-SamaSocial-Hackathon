# Fertilizer Subsidy Reform
The restructuring of fertilizer subsidy programs to support more efficient and timely use of fertilizers rather than simply increasing their use. In low-input systems, limited subsidies may be justified to increase yields and enhance soil carbon, while in high-input systems, subsidies may encourage overuse that harms the environment.
