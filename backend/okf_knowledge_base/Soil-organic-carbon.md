# Soil organic carbon
The carbon component of soil organic matter, crucial for soil health, fertility, and carbon sequestration. The text highlights its importance for mitigating climate change, bolstering adaptation, improving soil quality, and enhancing the efficiency of nitrogen and potassium use.
