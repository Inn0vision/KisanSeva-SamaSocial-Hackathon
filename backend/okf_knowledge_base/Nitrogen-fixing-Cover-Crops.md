# Nitrogen-fixing Cover Crops
Nitrogen-fixing cover crops are plants that enhance soil fertility by converting atmospheric nitrogen into a form usable by plants. The text mentions them as a practice that can increase yields and contribute to both energy efficiency and sustainable increases in productivity and income, especially when combined with practices like reduced or zero tillage. They offer an alternative to synthetic nitrogen fertilizers, thereby reducing fossil fuel-dependent inputs and associated GHG emissions.
