# Pest-Resistant Crops
The introduction of new pest-resistant [[Wheat]] varieties is highlighted as an adaptation measure to expected future growing conditions, particularly in the Huang-Huai-Hai Plain of China. This strategy aims to reduce crop losses and enhance food security under changing climates.
