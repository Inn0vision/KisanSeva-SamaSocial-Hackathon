# Dredges
A type of active fishing gear used in small-scale fishing for shellfish species, which is a key source of higher-value market supply but has higher capital and energy needs. It is also used in commercial fishing for important demersal and shellfish species. Climate change can cause changing species mixes and require more effort per catch. Response strategies include adapting gear, timing, adjusting management, and reducing energy costs.
