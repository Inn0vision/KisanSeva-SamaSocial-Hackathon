# Climate Change, Agriculture and Food Security (CCAFS)
A CGIAR research programme focused on addressing the interlinked challenges of climate change, agricultural development, and food security. CCAFS, in collaboration with FAO, published a training guide for collecting and analyzing gender-sensitive information in agricultural communities facing climate change. It also created the Adaptation and Mitigation Knowledge Network (AMKN), an online, map-based platform.
