# Herbicide
Herbicides are chemical inputs used to control [[Weeds]]. Overuse of herbicides can lead to the emergence of herbicide-tolerant varieties of [[Weeds]], creating new challenges for weed management. Sustainable intensification promotes careful targeting of external inputs.
