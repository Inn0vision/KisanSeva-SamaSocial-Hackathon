# Genetic Resources (Crops)
Genetic resources, including traditional and improved crop varieties and their wild relatives, are crucial for adaptation to climate change. Preserving diverse genetic material increases the resilience potential of the whole agricultural system, allowing for the development of varieties adapted to new conditions like drought or increased incidence of [[Agricultural Pests]] and [[Agricultural Diseases]]. Access to these resources, facilitated by effective "seed systems," is essential for farmers.
