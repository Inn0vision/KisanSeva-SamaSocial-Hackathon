# Livestock
Pastoral areas, where livestock are raised, are vulnerable to more frequent drying of water points and greater variability in available [[Animal Feed]] due to climate change. Integrating livestock with crops, fish, and trees enhances farming system resilience. Improved [[Pastures]] management and the use of feed additives that suppress [[Methane Emissions]] from [[Ruminants]] are strategies to mitigate climate impact.
