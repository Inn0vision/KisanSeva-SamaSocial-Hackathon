# Partially fed inland/coastal pond systems
A mainly inland, intensified aquaculture system with increased stocks and yields. It employs a range of practices and species mixes, with water exchange and aeration. This system is susceptible to slight changes in temperature ranges, salinity, water exchange, productivity, and species performance. Response strategies involve adjusting stocking, feeding, and harvest strategies, improving water management, intensifying monitoring and early warning systems, and possibly changing the species mix.
