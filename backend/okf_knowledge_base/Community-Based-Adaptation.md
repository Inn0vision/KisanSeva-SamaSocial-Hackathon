# Community-Based Adaptation
A community-led process, based on communities’ priorities, needs, knowledge, and capacities, which should empower people to plan for and cope with the impacts of climate change. It has been applied to promote adaptation in the agriculture sector and shares a similar bottom-up, participatory approach with Community-Based Disaster Risk Management (CBDRM). CBA often involves bringing scientific knowledge and climate change data to communities.
