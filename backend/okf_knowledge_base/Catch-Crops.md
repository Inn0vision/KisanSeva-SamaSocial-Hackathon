# Catch Crops
Crops grown between two main crops, primarily to prevent nutrient leaching, reduce erosion, and improve soil health. The text refers to "Catch crops and green manures as biological tools in nitrogen management in temperate zones," emphasizing their role in sustainable agriculture.
