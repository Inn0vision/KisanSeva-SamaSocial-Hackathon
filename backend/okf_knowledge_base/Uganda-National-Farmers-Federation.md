# Uganda National Farmers Federation
The Uganda National Farmers Federation (UNFFE) is a national farmers' union established in 1992. It has over 200,000 individual farmers and more than 70 organizations as members. It provides support through policy advocacy, agricultural trade fairs, market and climate information, and extension services (e.g., publications, radio sessions, workshops). In response to climate change, UNFFE organized workshops on "Climate Adaptive Approaches to Food Security" to identify adaptation measures and mainstream CSA into its support systems.
