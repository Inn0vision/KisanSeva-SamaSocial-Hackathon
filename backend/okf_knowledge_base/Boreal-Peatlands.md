# Boreal Peatlands
Boreal peatlands are a type of [[Peat Soils]] found in boreal regions. When converted to cropland, emissions from these peatlands are around 7 tonnes of carbon per hectare per year, indicating their contribution to carbon emissions upon land use change, though at a lower rate than tropical peatlands.
