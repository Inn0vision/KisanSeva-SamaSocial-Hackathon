# Conservation Tillage
A Climate-Smart Agriculture practice that stores moisture and organic matter in the soil, increasing adaptive capacity to drought while supporting climate change mitigation through carbon sequestration. Example provided: Over half of Brazil's soybean crop is farmed using conservation tillage.
