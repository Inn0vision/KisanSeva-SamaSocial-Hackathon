# Late Blight
Late blight is a fungal disease caused by *Phytophthora infestans*, the same pathogen responsible for the Irish potato famine in the 1800s. It is a devastating disease for potato crops. Climate change is expected to cause the expansion of late blight into geographical areas that were previously unaffected, posing a significant new threat to global potato production.
