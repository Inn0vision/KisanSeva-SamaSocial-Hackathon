# Disaster Risk Management (DRM)
A proactive approach that can be applied to anticipate and respond to the complete climate change impact profile. The concept of "building back better" originated in DRM to reflect the need to not only respond to emergencies but to prepare for future ones. Climate-smart DRM is advocated for integration into national strategies to build resilience in the fisheries and aquaculture sector.
