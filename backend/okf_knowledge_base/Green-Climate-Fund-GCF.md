# Green Climate Fund (GCF)
A fund established under the UNFCCC to serve as an operating entity of its financial mechanism. It was formally created to channel a significant portion of the US$100 billion per year pledged by developed countries by 2020 for climate action in developing countries. It is envisioned to provide financing for both mitigation and adaptation and has the potential to fundamentally change the architecture of global climate finance. Its operational details, including funding areas and priorities, were under discussion at the time of the text.
