# Agrochemicals
A general term encompassing chemical products used in agriculture, including pesticides and fertilizers. Irrigated agriculture, being more intensively managed, typically uses greater amounts of inorganic fertilizer and other agrochemicals compared to rainfed systems. Efforts to reduce greenhouse gases through improved crop management practices are likely to have a greater impact in irrigated lands where these inputs are used more heavily. Pollution control is also mentioned as a supply-side option for coping with water scarcity, which can be related to agrochemical runoff.
