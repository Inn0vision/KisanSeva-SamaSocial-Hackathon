# Green Manures
Green manures are crops grown specifically to be incorporated into the soil to improve its fertility and structure. They boost Soil Organic Matter (SOM) and labile carbon, and are a technique used in organic and low external input agriculture to enhance soil quality and provide nutrients, especially nitrogen. However, the text also notes that they can disturb soil structure and do not maintain a protective litter or plant cover.
