# Disease
Diseases are a significant cause of food loss and waste. For vegetable commodities, disease damage is a cause of loss during production. For livestock, diseases such as swine fevers and tick-borne diseases during breeding can lead to animal death. Zoonotic diseases, which can pass from animals to humans, are also highlighted as a critical concern in livestock production.
