# Halophytic Plants
Halophytic plants are salt-tolerant plants that can grow in conditions of high soil salinity. The text suggests that growing halophytic plants is an option for agricultural lands affected by high levels of soil salinity, which is a progressive cause of soil degradation exacerbated by climate change.
