# Maize
Maize is a traditional crop cultivated by smallholder farmers in the Baramulla, Bandipora, and Pulwama districts of Kashmir, India. Farmers face increasing challenges with its cultivation due to increasingly unpredictable rains, snowfall, temperatures, and humidity, leading to repeated crop failures and rising debt. This has made maize cultivation economically unviable for many, forcing them to abandon farming.
