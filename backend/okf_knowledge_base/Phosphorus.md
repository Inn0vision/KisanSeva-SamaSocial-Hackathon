# Phosphorus
Phosphorus is an essential [[Soil Nutrients]] required for plant growth. While not as extensively discussed as nitrogen or potassium in the provided text, it is mentioned as one of the macro- and micronutrients essential for plant growth, released during the decomposition of [[Soil Organic Matter (SOM)]]. It is also implicitly part of the salts that can accumulate during [[Soil Salinization]].
