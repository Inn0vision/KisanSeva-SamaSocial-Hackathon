# Viet Nam
Once a food-deficit country, it is now a major surplus producer and global rice exporter. The text notes that it still regulates rice production, requiring it to be grown on certain areas due to historical food security concerns, despite its current export status. Paddy rice production is a major source of the country's agricultural GHG emissions.
