# Crop Genetic Resources
Crop genetic resources refer to the genetic base of crops, which is vital for increasing the likelihood of species having traits necessary to fulfill functional roles in ecosystems and deliver services in a changing climate. The text notes that genetic resources are being lost through extinction and crop genetic resource loss. Maintaining high levels of genetic diversity, including traditional crop varieties, is fundamental to decrease risk and maintain stability.
