# Animal Diseases
Animal diseases are expected to be impacted by climate change, though models often do not fully account for these effects. Improving animal health, including disease prevention and management, is crucial for livestock system efficiency, food security, and preparedness against new risks. Proper nutrition also helps protect animals from diseases.

# Animal Diseases
A general term for diseases affecting livestock. Climate change is expected to indirectly impact livestock through the emergence and increased incidence of animal diseases, as higher temperatures and changed rainfall patterns can alter the abundance, distribution, and transmission of [[Animal Pathogens]]. Disease outbreaks can destabilize food supply from livestock.
