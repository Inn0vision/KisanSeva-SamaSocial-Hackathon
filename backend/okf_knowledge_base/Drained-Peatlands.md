# Drained Peatlands
Drained peatlands are [[Peatlands]] where water levels have been lowered, often for forestry, [[Food Crops]], or [[Cash Crops]] like [[Palm Oil]]. This drainage causes significant GHG emissions and increases the risk of fires, being responsible for almost one-quarter of carbon emissions from the land-use sector. Rewetting drained peatlands is a primary approach to reduce these emissions.
