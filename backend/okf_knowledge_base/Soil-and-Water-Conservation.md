# Soil and Water Conservation
A set of practices aimed at protecting soil from erosion and managing water resources efficiently. Techniques include building physical structures like terraces and irrigation channels, as well as biological methods like agroforestry and area enclosures. These are common activities in safety net public works programs (e.g., FFA, PSNP) because they enhance land productivity, improve water availability, and increase resilience to climate shocks, making them fundamental to Climate-Smart Agriculture.
