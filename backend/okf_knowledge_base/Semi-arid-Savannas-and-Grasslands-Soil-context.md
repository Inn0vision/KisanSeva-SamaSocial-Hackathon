# Semi-arid Savannas and Grasslands (Soil context)
Semi-arid savannas and grasslands are agricultural systems located in semi-arid climates. These areas are identified as vulnerable lands where [[Soil Organic Carbon (SOC)]] stocks are less resilient. When native vegetation or pastures in these environments are converted to cropland, soil carbon stocks can decrease by as much as 30 percent in less than five years, indicating a rapid loss of carbon upon land use change.
