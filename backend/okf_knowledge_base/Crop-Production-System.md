# Crop Production System
Crop production systems refer to the methods and practices involved in cultivating plants for food, feed, and other agricultural purposes. The text emphasizes the need for climate-smart crop production systems to sustainably increase agricultural productivity and incomes, adapt to climate change, and reduce greenhouse gas emissions. This includes managing natural biological processes, enhancing resource efficiency in plant production, and utilizing genetic resources effectively. The concept is central to achieving global food security amidst a growing population and the adverse impacts of climate change.
