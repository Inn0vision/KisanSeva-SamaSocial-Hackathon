# Chicle Gum
Chicle gum, derived from latex, is a non-timber forest product that can be harvested in the multiple-use zones of the Maya Biosphere Reserve under regulated conditions. Historically, latex extraction from the [[Sapodilla Tree (Manilkara zapota)]] was a major economy.
