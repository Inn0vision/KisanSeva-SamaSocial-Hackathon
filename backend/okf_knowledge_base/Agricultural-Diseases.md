# Agricultural Diseases
Agricultural diseases are identified as a major risk to agricultural production, alongside [[Agricultural Pests]]. The text notes that climate change is expected to increase the incidence of diseases. It also mentions that drought can increase the vulnerability of cattle to diseases, highlighting the interconnectedness of environmental stressors and disease susceptibility. Preserving genetic resources is also crucial for developing disease-resistant varieties.
