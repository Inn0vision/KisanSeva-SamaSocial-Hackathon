# Seines
A type of active fishing gear used in small-scale fishing. This gear is part of systems that are a key source of higher-value market supply but have higher capital and energy needs. Climate impacts include changing species mix, timing, and locations, requiring more effort per catch. Response strategies involve adapting gear, timing, adjusting management, and working to reduce energy costs.
