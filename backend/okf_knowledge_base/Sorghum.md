# Sorghum
Sorghum is noted as a dominant crop in the Sahel region, cultivated by farmers on rehabilitated land in areas like Burkina Faso and Niger, alongside [[Millet]], [[Cowpea]], and [[Sesame]].

# Sorghum
A major cereal crop, one of the five species (along with [[Rice]], [[Wheat]], [[Maize]], and [[Millet]]) that collectively provide about 60% of the world's energy intake.
