# Truffles
Truffles are a type of fungi that grow in some areas of the Syrian Badia. Their gathering provides a significant potential for income generation for the local population, particularly for women, boosting family incomes. In 2010, a community with a 100,000-ha grazing area could earn up to USD1 million through the sale of truffles. This increased income contributes to economic autonomy and improved household well-being.
