# Farmer Cooperatives
Formal institutions formed by producers to improve their collective bargaining power, access to markets, and sharing of knowledge and resources. The text cites them as a key type of local institution that can support climate-smart agriculture. Examples given include the Jammu & Kashmir MAP Growers’ Cooperative, which helps farmers adapt to climate change by growing medicinal plants.
