# Plan Vert (Green Plan)
Morocco's integrated programme providing incentives for Climate-Smart Agriculture. It takes an integrated approach to river basin management, addressing water scarcity with a dual strategy: in irrigated areas, it incentivizes improved water management and links to international markets; in rain-fed areas, it increases access to social services, supports natural resource management, and promotes replacing arable crops with drought-tolerant trees like olives.
