# Barley
Studies conducted in the Finnish project ADACAPA have shown that regions growing more diverse varieties of barley have a higher average yield compared to areas growing a single variety. This finding supports the hypothesis that increasing diversity enhances the resilience and adaptive capacity of agrifood systems.
