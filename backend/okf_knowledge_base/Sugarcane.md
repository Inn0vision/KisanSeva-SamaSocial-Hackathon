# Sugarcane
Sugarcane is projected to benefit from climate change, with more favorable conditions leading to a considerable increase in its production area. This is in contrast to most other crops studied in Brazil.

# Sugarcane
A major cash crop, particularly in Brazil, where sustainability in its supply chain (farm, mill, and refining processes) is a key priority. It is a focal point for corporate sustainability partnerships, such as that between The Coca-Cola Company and WWF, which aims to promote environmental stewardship. Bonsucro certification is identified as a means of ensuring increased sustainability for sugarcane production.
