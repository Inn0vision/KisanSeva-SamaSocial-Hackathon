# Carbon Markets
Systems for trading certificates or credits representing a reduction of greenhouse gas emissions. Includes compliance markets (e.g., under the Kyoto Protocol) and voluntary carbon markets. The share of REDD+ certificates in voluntary markets is an important indicator for the carbon trading potential of forest carbon. The trading of agricultural carbon credits is highly unlikely to develop into a significant source of Climate-Smart Agriculture financing in developing countries in the mid-term future, with the exception of established niches like methane avoidance from manure management. Carbon markets in general are facing great challenges today.
