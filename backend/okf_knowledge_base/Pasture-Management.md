# Pasture Management
Measures involving the sowing of improved varieties of [[Pasture]], including perennial fodders, pastures, and [[Legumes (as feed crops)]], and intensification through fertilization, cutting regimes, and irrigation practices to enhance productivity, [[Soil Organic Carbon]], pasture quality, and animal performance.
