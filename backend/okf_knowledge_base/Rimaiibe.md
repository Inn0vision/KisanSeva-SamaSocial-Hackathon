# Rimaiibe
An ethnic group in Northern Burkina Faso, specifically in the village of Biidi 2. Historically, they were effectively slaves to the Fulbe until postcolonial legislation in the 1980s afforded them rights and land. They have successfully adapted to climate change through strategies like labour migration, working for development projects, growing gardens, and including women in economic activity—practices that are often contravened by Fulbe cultural norms.
