# Intensive tank and raceway systems
A highly intensified and controlled system with high water exchange, mainly used for hatcheries and high-value fish, both inland and coastal. It faces localized management issues related to temperature and water supply. Response strategies involve modifying production cycles and feed regimes, implementing more water treatment, and improving health and hygiene management. A shift towards full water recycle is noted, though flow-through ongrowing is less common.
