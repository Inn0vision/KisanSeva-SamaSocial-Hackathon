# Parasites
Organisms that live on or in a host organism, deriving nutrients at the host's expense. Resistance or tolerance to parasites is an important trait for genetic resources in adapting to climate change, as climate change can affect the distribution and abundance of disease vectors and the epidemiology of many crop and livestock diseases.
