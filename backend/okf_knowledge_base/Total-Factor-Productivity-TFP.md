# Total Factor Productivity (TFP)
An economic measure of resource-use efficiency, calculated as the ratio of agricultural output to the combined inputs used in production. It is a fundamental building block of climate-smart agriculture (CSA) as it represents growth driven by efficiency gains rather than area expansion or increased input use. In sub-Saharan Africa, TFP as a source of growth has decreased since 2001.
