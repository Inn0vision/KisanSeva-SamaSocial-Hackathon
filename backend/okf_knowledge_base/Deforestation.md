# Deforestation
The clearing of trees, transforming a forest into cleared land. It is identified as a form of land degradation and a driver of ecosystem stress that contributes to underlying disaster risk. Addressing such drivers is a goal of both Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) and Climate Change Adaptation (CCA).
