# National Adaptation Programmes of Action (NAPA)
A process established under the UNFCCC for Least Developed Countries (LDCs) to identify priority activities that respond to their urgent and immediate needs to adapt to climate change. The preparation of NAPAs was financed by the Least Developed Countries Fund (LDCF). These programmes often prioritize agricultural adaptation due to the sector's high vulnerability.
