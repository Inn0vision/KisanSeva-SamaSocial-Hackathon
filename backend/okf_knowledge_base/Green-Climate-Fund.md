# Green Climate Fund
A fund established within the framework of the UNFCCC to assist developing countries in adaptation and mitigation practices to counter climate change. Its current design process might be influenced by the overall dynamic of increasing attention on integrative approaches like Climate-Smart Agriculture. A clear focus on CSA embedded in the GCF design would significantly impact how CSA approaches can be realized and scaled-up. It is assumed the GCF will have a significant impact on the entire climate finance landscape, both in structure and in prioritization principles.
