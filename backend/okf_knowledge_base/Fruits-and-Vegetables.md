# Fruits and Vegetables
Fruits and vegetables are identified as products with significant regional differences in food loss patterns. In Africa, processing and distribution are weak links for these products, while in Europe, most losses occur at the production and consumption stages. They are also categorized as "fresh perishable products" that often require cold chains and rapid transport.
