# Forest Lands
Forest lands are areas covered by forests. The text mentions that the overexploitation or clearance of wooded and forest lands is an unsustainable land management practice that reduces their productive capacities. Planting trees is highlighted as a practice that can introduce organic matter at greater depth in the soil and reduce wind erosion.
