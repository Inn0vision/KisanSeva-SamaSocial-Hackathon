# Degraded soils
Soils that have lost their quality and productivity due to various factors like erosion, nutrient depletion, or compaction. The text discusses the restoration of degraded soils through increases in soil organic carbon pools to improve production and foster food security. It also mentions the reduction of degraded pastures in silvopastoral systems.
