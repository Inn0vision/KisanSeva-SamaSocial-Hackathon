# Bunds/Zai
A water management practice involving the construction of small barriers or pits to capture rainwater and increase soil moisture, thereby potentially increasing yields. It can reduce variability in dry areas but may increase damage due to heavy rains if constructed primarily to increase soil moisture without adequate drainage considerations.
