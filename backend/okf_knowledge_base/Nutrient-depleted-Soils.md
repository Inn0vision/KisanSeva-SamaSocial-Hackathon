# Nutrient-depleted Soils
Nutrient-depleted soils are those lacking essential plant nutrients. Nitrogenous fertilizers are particularly beneficial for increasing productivity in these soils. Integrated soil fertility management, combining organic matter and fertilizers, is a strategy to address or prevent macro- and micro-nutrient deficiencies in such soils, especially in tropical and subtropical regions with quick SOM turnover.
