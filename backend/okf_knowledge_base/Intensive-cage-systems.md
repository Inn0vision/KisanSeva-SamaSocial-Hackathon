# Intensive cage systems
A widespread global sector, mainly involving fish, that is intensive and fully fed with a wide range of systems and species. This system is vulnerable to temperature, salinity, and oxygen level changes, as well as algal blooms and pathogen interactions. Response strategies include better stock, feed, and disease management, proper (risk-based) site selection, some relocations, and monitoring environmental conditions and early warning systems.
