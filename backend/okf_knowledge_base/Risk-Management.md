# Risk Management
The process of understanding, assessing, and addressing risks to reduce their negative impact on agricultural systems. Climate change increases risks and uncertainty for farmers, which can lead to altered investment patterns such as choosing low-risk subsistence crops, reduced input application, lower technology adoption, and overall lower investment. Essential strategies for managing risk in a CSA context include information generation and dissemination (e.g., climate forecasts), agricultural insurance, safety nets (social protection programmes), diversification of income sources, and enhancing ecosystem services to build resilience.
