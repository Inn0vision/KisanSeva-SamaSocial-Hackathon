# Floating Raft System
An aquaculture technique used for mussel farming, particularly in Galicia, Spain. The rafts or floating platforms are a rectangular wood frame of about 500 m². Usually, one raft constitutes one farm and is owned by a single family. This system benefits from high natural phytoplankton production.
