# AdapCC Initiative
The 'Adaptation for Smallholders to Climate Change' (AdapCC) initiative is a public-private partnership between Cafédirect (a British Fairtrade company) and the German Agency for International Cooperation (GIZ). It supports coffee and tea farmers in developing strategies to cope with climate change risks. The initiative was piloted in six countries and involved conducting Risk and Opportunity Assessments (ROA) and developing action plans that included training, establishing meteorological stations, and testing water management techniques.
