# Conservation Agriculture
An approach to farm management that aims to achieve sustainable and profitable agriculture and aims at conserving and improving natural resources. It is linked to "[[Soil Organic Carbon]] accumulation and greenhouse gas emission reductions."

# Conservation Agriculture
An approach to managing agro-ecosystems that is promoted for both risk reduction and climate change adaptation. It is particularly suitable for areas with low soil productivity that are exposed to difficult climatic conditions like drought and rainfall irregularity. It aims to improve and sustain productivity and food security while preserving and enhancing the resource base and the environment. It is an example of a practice that serves multiple functions within Climate-Smart Agriculture.
