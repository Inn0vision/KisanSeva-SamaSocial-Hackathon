# Commercial fishing
A fishing sector involving larger-scale operations, including driftnet, long-line, dredge, trawl, purse-seine, and freezer/factory vessel fishing. It targets key pelagic, demersal, and shellfish species, often on the open seas or in localized regions with international scope. This sector is impacted by climate change through changes in species distribution, increased effort per catch, and higher risks. Response strategies include improving stock monitoring and location efficiency, moving to higher value markets, adjusting operations, and in some cases, reducing capacity or decommissioning vessels.
