# Flooded Rice Production Soils (Paddy)"
Flooded rice production, commonly known as paddy cultivation, is specifically identified as a source of methane (CH4) emissions, contributing to greenhouse gases. The text suggests that avoiding flooding or managing it judiciously in paddy rice systems can help reduce methane emissions as a climate change mitigation strategy.
