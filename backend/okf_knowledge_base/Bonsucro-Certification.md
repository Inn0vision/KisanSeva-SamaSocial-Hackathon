# Bonsucro Certification
A globally recognized, third-party certification standard for sustainably produced sugarcane. Developed through an independent, multi-stakeholder initiative, it provides a mechanism for achieving sustainable production in respect of economic, social, and environmental dimensions. It is a key tool for companies seeking to advance sustainable agriculture practices in their sugarcane supply chains.
