# Forage
Forage refers to plant material (mainly plant leaves and stems) eaten by grazing livestock. Improving forage productivity and quality, through better composition (e.g., with [[Legumes]]) and management, is crucial for enhancing livestock efficiency, reducing methane emissions, and adapting to climate change.

# Forage
Forage refers to plant material (mainly plant leaves and stems) eaten by grazing livestock. In the Ruoergai peatlands, it is noted for early plant growth, higher productivity, and better nutrient availability, making peatlands preferred grazing pastures. Forage cultivation is also supported for peatland restoration.
