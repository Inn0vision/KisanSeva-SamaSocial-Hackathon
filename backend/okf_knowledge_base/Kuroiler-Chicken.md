# Kuroiler Chicken
A robust, improved dual-purpose backyard chicken breed developed in India. It lays 100-150 eggs per year (compared to 40 from native 'Desi' breeds) and grows to 2.5 kg in about half the time. The meat is darker and more flavorful, commanding a price premium. The birds are adapted to scavenge for food, have higher food conversion ratios, and can survive long droughts with limited nutrition, making them climate-resilient.
