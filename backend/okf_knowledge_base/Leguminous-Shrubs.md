# Leguminous Shrubs
Shrubs that are also legumes, capable of fixing atmospheric nitrogen. The text mentions "agroforestry systems that integrate compatible leguminous shrubs and trees with crops restore SOM and N through the leaf litter, help fix N through symbiotic Rhizobium."
