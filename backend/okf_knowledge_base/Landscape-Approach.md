# Landscape Approach
An integrated approach to managing various land-based ecosystem services that requires coordination across sectors and stakeholders. This approach may involve trade-offs that need to be explicitly quantified and addressed through negotiated solutions. Essential for the better management of agricultural production and ecosystem services within Climate-Smart Agriculture frameworks.
