# Tea
Organic tea farms are mentioned in the context of business schemes in Bangladesh that promote household biomass production for energy. These farms provide funding for cattle-leasing programs and biogas systems, receiving dung and slurry (biofertilizer) in return.

# Tea
Tea is mentioned as a crop supported by the AdapCC initiative, which helps tea farmers in East Africa (Kenya, Uganda, and the United Republic of Tanzania) develop strategies to cope with the risks and impacts of climate change.
