# Verified Carbon Standard
A major voluntary carbon market standard that allows for carbon credits from Climate-Smart Agriculture activities. It has approved a carbon accounting methodology for sustainable agricultural land management developed for the Kenya Agricultural Carbon Project, making these credits tradable on the voluntary carbon market.
