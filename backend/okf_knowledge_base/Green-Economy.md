# Green Economy
An economic system that results in improved human well-being and social equity, while significantly reducing environmental risks and ecological scarcities. For agriculture, this means economically viable sustainable practices (crops, livestock, forestry, fisheries and aquaculture) that help conserve land, water, plant and animal genetic resources, biodiversity and ecosystems, while enhancing resilience to climate change and natural disasters. Climate-Smart Agriculture is a concrete way to operationalize sustainable development within a green economy framework.
