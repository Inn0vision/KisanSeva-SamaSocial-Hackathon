# Sodic Soils
Sodic soils are a type of [[Alkaline Soils]] where the sodium ion predominates. They present particular challenges due to their very poor [[Soil Structure]], which severely limits or prevents water infiltration and drainage. This poor structure exacerbates the risk of [[Soil Erosion]]. Sodic conditions are a consequence of [[Soil Salinization]] and contribute to [[Soil Degradation]].

# Sodic Soils
Sodic soils are mentioned as one of the diverse soil types that need to be considered in climate-smart agriculture. They are related to the broader issue of soil and water salinity in agricultural landscapes.
