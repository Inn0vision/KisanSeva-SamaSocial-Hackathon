# Ocean Acidification
A major climate change threat for all bivalve farming. It is characterized by lower levels of pH in the water, which can interfere with the formation of the calcium-rich shells (valves) and other physiological processes in mussels and similar organisms. Adaptation measures to this threat may involve selecting resistant strains of mussels and increasing hatchery-produced larvae under more controlled conditions.
