# Vessel Day Scheme (VDS)
A scheme used by the Parties to the Nauru Agreement (PNA) to control and distribute fishing effort for tuna in the western Pacific. It sets a total allowable effort within PNA waters, allocated among member exclusive economic zones (EEZs) based on historical fishing patterns. Members can trade fishing days to accommodate shifts in tuna distribution caused by climatic events like the El Niño Southern Oscillation (ENSO). This cap-and-trade-like system provides flexibility to adapt to both climatic variation and projected long-term redistribution of tuna due to climate change.
