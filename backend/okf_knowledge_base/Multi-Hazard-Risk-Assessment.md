# Multi-Hazard Risk Assessment
A tool that involves identifying the spatial distribution of hazards (climate-related and geological) at different scales, assessing the exposure and vulnerability of systems (e.g., farming systems) to these hazards, and evaluating the overall level of risk. It is presented as a critical tool for informing the design of appropriate Climate-Smart Agriculture (CSA) initiatives. When combined with downscaled climate change scenarios, it provides a harmonized framework for assessing all spatially relevant hazards in specific geographic areas, facilitating short- and long-term holistic planning for CSA.
