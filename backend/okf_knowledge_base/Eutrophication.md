# Eutrophication
A process discussed as a potential impact of climate change and human activity on aquatic systems. It is also an output measured in environmental impact assessments of food production systems (eutrophication potential). In aquaculture, eutrophication and harmful algal blooms are cited as impact areas requiring better planning, farm siting, monitoring, and emergency procedures.
