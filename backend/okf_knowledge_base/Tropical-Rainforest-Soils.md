# Tropical Rainforest Soils
Tropical rainforest soils are found under tropical rainforest ecosystems. The text highlights that cultivation of these soils causes significant losses, with more than 60 percent of original [[Soil Organic Carbon (SOC)]] stocks being lost in just a few years after conversion. This makes them vulnerable to high SOM losses upon land conversion.
