# Purse-seine fishing
A commercial fishing method important for major pelagic stocks in key regions, mainly marine. It is highly efficient when effectively targeted. Climate change can cause changes in distribution across wider zonal areas and increase the time and effort required per catch. Response strategies include improving stock monitoring and location options, moving to higher market quality and values, and possibly implementing capacity reduction.
