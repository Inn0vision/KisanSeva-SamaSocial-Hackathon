# Cropland
Cropland refers to land used for growing crops. The text highlights that semi-arid savannas, grasslands, tropical rainforests, and peatlands are being converted to arable land and cropland at increasing rates, leading to significant losses of Soil Organic Carbon (SOC). A large percentage of the world's cropland was estimated to be degraded in the 1990s. Sustainable management practices are needed to reduce land conversion and promote sustainable use of cropland.
