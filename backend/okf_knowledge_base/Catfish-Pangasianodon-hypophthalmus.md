# Catfish (Pangasianodon hypophthalmus)
A species of catfish farmed in the Mekong Delta of Viet Nam, also known as tra, sutchi, or Pangasius. It is a highly productive aquaculture system, with global averages of 250 to 400 tonnes per hectare per crop. The sector employs over 170,000 people and generates significant export income. It is characterized by a comparatively lower carbon footprint and efficient use of inputs like fish meal.
