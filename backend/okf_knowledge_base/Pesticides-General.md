# Pesticides (General)"
Chemical or biological agents used to control pests. The [[Green Revolution]] was characterized by the widespread use of a "chemical package," which included pesticides. While effective in increasing yields, intensive use has contributed to environmental pollution.

In the context of sustainable agriculture and climate-smart practices, there is an emphasis on reducing the application of pesticides and associated risks, particularly to protect beneficial organisms like pollinators. "Low risk pesticides" are mentioned as part of a rational combination of pest control methods for managing [[Striga]]. The [[System of Rice Intensification (SRI)]] has also shown reduced need for chemical fertilizers and no insect or disease infestation, implying a reduced reliance on such inputs.
