# Cash-for-Work
A safety net intervention where participants receive cash payments for work on public projects, often in post-disaster or rehabilitation contexts. The cash provides immediate income for affected households while the work focuses on repairing critical infrastructure, such as irrigation channels, to restore agricultural productivity and build resilience against future shocks.
