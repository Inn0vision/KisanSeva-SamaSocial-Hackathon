# FAO Code of Conduct for Responsible Fisheries
Demonstrates principles and standards applicable to the conservation, management, and development of the world’s fisheries, including aquaculture. These principles and standards cover issues such as the prevention of overfishing, the minimization of negative impacts to aquatic ecosystems and local communities, and the protection of human rights for a secure and just livelihood. It provides the overarching principles for sustainable approaches to expanding output in fisheries and aquaculture.
