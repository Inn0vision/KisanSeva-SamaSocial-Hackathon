# International Food Policy Research Institute (IFPRI)
A major international agricultural research institute. It is noted for developing tools and conducting research to inform agricultural policy, including the Women’s Empowerment in Agriculture Index (WEAI). IFPRI also developed the Net-Map social networking tool for assessing governance systems.
