# Integrated Pest Management (IPM)
A project (Promoting Integrated Pest Management Project - PIPM) focused on building the capacity of farmers for healthy crops, more yields, and more incomes. It represents a holistic approach to pest control, aiming to minimize economic, health, and environmental risks.
