# Small-scale fishing
A significant global fishing sector, mainly coastal, that is a major source of market and domestic supply. It is characterized by low capital and energy needs when using passive gear, and higher capital and energy needs when using active gear. This sector encompasses a range of gears, including traps, lines, gill nets, longlines, dredges, seines, and trawls. It is highly vulnerable to climate change impacts, including shifting species mixes, locations, timing, and increased fishing risks. Response strategies include adapting gear, timing, access, developing alternative markets, adjusting management, and in cases of major disruption, potentially shifting to alternative livelihoods.
