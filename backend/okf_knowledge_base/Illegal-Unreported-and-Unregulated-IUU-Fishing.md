# Illegal, Unreported and Unregulated (IUU) Fishing
A continued challenge of governance issues in fisheries resources, cited as a distinct characteristic of the sector. It represents substantial levels of fishing activity that operate outside of regulatory frameworks, contributing to overfishing and conflicted management environments.
