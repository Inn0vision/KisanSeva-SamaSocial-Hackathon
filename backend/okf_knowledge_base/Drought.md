# Drought
A period of abnormally dry weather long enough to cause a serious hydrological imbalance. It is specifically listed as a climate-related hazard that affects food security. Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) and Climate Change Adaptation (CCA) efforts aim to address the increased risk from climate-change related processes like drought.
