# Vegetable Commodities
Refers to fresh fruits and vegetables, which are among the most lost and wasted foods due to their highly perishable nature. Losses occur throughout the value chain, from production (e.g., poor practices, pest damage) to post-harvest handling, storage, and retail. The text details specific causes of loss and interventions, such as improved harvesting practices, better packaging, and investment in cooling facilities.
