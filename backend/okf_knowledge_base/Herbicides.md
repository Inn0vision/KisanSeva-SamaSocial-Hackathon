# Herbicides
Herbicides are a specific type of pesticide used for weed management. The text mentions that where no-till cultivation is used without cover crops and relies on herbicides to manage weeds, the positive effects on nitrogen uptake and reduced leaching, as well as on yields, may be less evident. Herbicides are also listed as one of the weed management options in integrated soil-crop-water management practices.

# Herbicides
Chemical substances used to control or kill unwanted plants (weeds). The text states that mechanized direct drill systems in conservation agriculture "may use herbicides to manage weeds," but also notes that there "may be slight negative GHG impacts from application of additional chemical herbicides for weed control instead of weed control by tillage."
