# Manila clam (Ruditapes philippinarum)
A species of clam farmed in integrated multitrophic aquaculture (IMTA) systems. It filter feeds on phytoplankton and organic detritus, acting as a carbon sink within the system. It is a key component in integrated shrimp culture models in Southeast China.
