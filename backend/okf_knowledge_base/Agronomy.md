# Agronomy
A branch of agricultural science dealing with field-crop production and soil management. As a CSA investment category, it involves developing improved crop varieties, multiplying breeder seed, increasing distribution of improved seed, and training staff and farmers in seed multiplication. It is a foundational practice for enhancing productivity and resilience.
