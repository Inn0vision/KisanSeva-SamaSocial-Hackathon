# Degraded Lands
The restoration of degraded lands is identified as a significant strategy for enhancing soil carbon sinks and improving ecosystem services. Improved grazing management can also contribute to the rehabilitation of degraded lands.

# Degraded Lands
Degraded lands are areas that have lost their productivity, often due to factors like severe drought, intensive grazing, or [[Soil Erosion]]. Restoring degraded lands and increasing the level of [[Soil Organic Matter]] in them is identified as a priority action for improving both efficiency and resilience. The text provides an example of restoring degraded [[Rangelands]] in the Syrian Arab Republic by reintroducing native plants.
