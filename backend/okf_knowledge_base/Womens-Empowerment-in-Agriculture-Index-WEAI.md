# Women’s Empowerment in Agriculture Index (WEAI)
A measurement tool developed by the International Food Policy Research Institute (IFPRI) and partners in 2012 to quantify women's empowerment relative to men within their households. The index assesses empowerment across five domains: inclusion in decisions about agricultural production; access to and decision-making power over productive resources; control over use of income; leadership in the community; and use of time. It was piloted in Bangladesh, Guatemala, and Uganda to understand different gender dynamics.
