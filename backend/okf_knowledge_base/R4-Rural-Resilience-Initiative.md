# R4 Rural Resilience Initiative
A partnership between WFP and Oxfam America that scales up an integrated approach to building farmers' resilience to climate risk. R4 combines four strategies: improved resource management (risk reduction), weather-index insurance (risk transfer), microcredit (prudent risk taking), and savings (risk reserves). A key innovation is allowing farmers to pay for insurance premiums with their labor ("insurance-for-work") through existing safety net programs, making insurance accessible to the most vulnerable.
