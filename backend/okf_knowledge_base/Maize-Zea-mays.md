# Maize (Zea mays)
A major global food crop, specifically mentioned as being highly susceptible to the parasitic weed [[Striga]] and the insect pest [[Maize Stem Borers]]. Damage from these pests is reduced when grown in healthy soils. Maize is also cultivated as a drought-tolerant crop using [[Conservation Agriculture]] practices, such as in Namibia, where yields have significantly increased.
