# Dry Arid and Semi-Arid Soils
Dry arid and semi-arid soils are found in regions with low and unpredictable rainfall, such as Uganda's Cattle Corridor. These landscapes are prone to droughts and are characterized by limited water availability, which challenges agricultural production but is well-suited for pastoralist livelihoods. Environmental degradation in these areas exacerbates their vulnerability to natural hazards.
