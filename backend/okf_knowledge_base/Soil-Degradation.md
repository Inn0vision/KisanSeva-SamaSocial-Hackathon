# Soil Degradation
Soil degradation refers to the decline in soil quality, often manifested as increased soil degradation due to erosion. The text mentions that the cultivation of [[Peatlands]] has in many cases led to their serious degradation. Minimizing drainage and limiting fertilizer use are strategies to reduce land degradation on [[Peatlands]].
