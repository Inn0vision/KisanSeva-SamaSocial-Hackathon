# Pond systems
A common method of aquaculture conducted in inland or coastal ponds. These systems range from extensive, traditionally unfed operations to completely fed, purpose-planned intensive operations. They are used for a variety of species, including fish and shrimp, and may be integrated with plants and animals. Climate change impacts include fluctuations in temperature, salinity, water exchange, productivity, and disease risks. Responses involve adjusting stocking, feeding, and harvest strategies, improving water management, and intensifying monitoring.
