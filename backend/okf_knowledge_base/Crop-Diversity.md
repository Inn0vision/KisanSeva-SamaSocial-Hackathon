# Crop Diversity
Maintaining crop diversity in production systems is crucial to avoid vulnerability and widespread crop loss resulting from particular biotic or abiotic threats. It is a key strategy for ensuring that agriculture can adapt to change and is a crucial component of promoting Climate-Smart Agriculture (CSA).
