# Grazing Management
The practice of optimizing grazing pressure on land to increase grassland productivity, deliver mitigation and adaptation benefits. Strategies include reducing grazing pressure to rehabilitate degraded lands and rotational grazing. It directly impacts the health and productivity of [[Pasture]] and [[Forage]].
