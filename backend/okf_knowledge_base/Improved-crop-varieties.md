# Improved crop varieties
A cropland management practice under CSA that involves developing and distributing improved seed varieties. This leads to increased crop yields and can reduce variability at the plot and local levels due to greater genetic diversity and adaptation to stress. It is a top-ranking investment in strategies like Malawi's ASWAp.
