# Payments for Ecosystem Services
An integrated approach to providing incentives for Climate-Smart Agriculture practices that generate environmental benefits. These payments recognize and reward the provision of public goods such as erosion control, water quality maintenance, carbon sequestration, and biodiversity conservation that result from sustainable agricultural practices.
