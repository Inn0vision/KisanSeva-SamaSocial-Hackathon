# Public Works Programmes
Labor-based safety net interventions that provide temporary employment, often with resource transfers as payment. These programs focus on creating or rehabilitating community assets, particularly those related to natural resource management and agricultural infrastructure (e.g., hillside terracing, irrigation repair, road construction). They provide insurance against shocks, increase current incomes, and generate sustainable benefits for household food security and community resilience, aligning with CSA goals.
