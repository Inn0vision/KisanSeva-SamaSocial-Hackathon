# Packaging
The use of materials to protect food during distribution, transport, and marketing. It provides protection against physical damage, insects, rodents, and other contaminants. The choice of material (e.g., paper, plastic, glass) involves trade-offs between cost, protection, public health, environmental impact, and consumer needs. Advanced solutions include modified atmosphere packaging and anti-microbial packaging.
