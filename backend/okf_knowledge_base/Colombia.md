# Colombia
A country with a new focus on integrated landscape planning for sustainable agriculture, forest intensification, and reduced deforestation. It has used a mix of policies and incentives to promote agrosilvopastoral systems, leading to sustainable increases in livestock productivity and a 'triple win' of productivity, resilience, and mitigation benefits.
