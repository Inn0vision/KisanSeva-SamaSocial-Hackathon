# Eco-labelling
A labelling system for consumer products that are deemed to have fewer negative impacts on the environment. In the context of fisheries and aquaculture, eco-labels could provide new market opportunities and increase the value added to products by certifying that they were produced following recognized energy-efficient or sustainable standards.
