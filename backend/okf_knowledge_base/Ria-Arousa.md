# Ria Arousa
The most productive area for mussel farming in Galicia, Spain. It benefits from an upwelling system that regularly contributes cold water and nutrients, causing exceptional phytoplankton production. This can sustain more than 2,000 floating rafts used for mussel cultivation. The estimated annual yield is about 60 tonnes per hectare without shell.
