# Climate-smart agriculture
An approach to transforming the agricultural sector to address current food security and climate change challenges. It focuses on three pillars: sustainably increasing agricultural productivity and incomes, adapting and building resilience to climate change, and reducing and/or removing greenhouse gas emissions.
