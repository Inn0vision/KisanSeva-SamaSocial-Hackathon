# Food Crops
Food crops are agricultural plants cultivated primarily for human consumption. The text notes a substantial increase in their production globally, but also highlights that their cultivation, especially on drained peatlands, can lead to serious degradation of these [[Soil Types]]. Paludiculture offers a sustainable method for producing food, such as [[Berries]], on rewetted [[Peatlands]].
