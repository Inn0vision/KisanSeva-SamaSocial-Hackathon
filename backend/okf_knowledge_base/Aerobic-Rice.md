# Aerobic Rice
A water management practice for [[Rice]] cultivation that involves avoiding continuous water saturation. This method is effective for mitigating [[Methane Emissions]] from rice fields. However, current yields tend to be poor (less than 2 tonnes per hectare), which acts as a strong disincentive for adoption, even when natural drainage conditions are favorable.

# Aerobic Rice
A method of growing rice in non-flooded, well-drained soil conditions, contrasting with traditional paddy systems. The text states that "irrigated aerobic rice and SRI do not require anaerobic conditions, it would appear that both practices can combine well with climate adaptation," implying benefits for water saving and reduced methane emissions.
