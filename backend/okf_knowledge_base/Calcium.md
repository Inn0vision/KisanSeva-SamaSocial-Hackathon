# Calcium
Calcium is an essential [[Soil Nutrients]] released into the soil by the decomposition of [[Soil Organic Matter (SOM)]]. It exists in the soil as a positively charged ion (cation) and is attracted by the negative charges on clay particles and SOM, contributing to the soil's nutrient reserve. Calcium is also mentioned as one of the salts that can accumulate in the surface soil layers during [[Soil Salinization]].
