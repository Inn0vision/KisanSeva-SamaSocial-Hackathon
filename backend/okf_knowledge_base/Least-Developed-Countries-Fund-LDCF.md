# Least Developed Countries Fund (LDCF)
A fund managed by the GEF under the UNFCCC, established to support least developed countries. Its original mandate was to finance the preparation of National Adaptation Programmes of Action (NAPAs). It has now shifted focus to implementing projects that respond to NAPA priorities. Agriculture is a main sector receiving LDCF funding, as it is highly vulnerable to climate change, making this fund a crucial source for the adaptation aspects of CSA in eligible countries.
