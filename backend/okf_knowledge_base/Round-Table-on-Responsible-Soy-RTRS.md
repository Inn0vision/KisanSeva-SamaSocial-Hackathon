# Round Table on Responsible Soy (RTRS)
A widely recognized certification standard for soy production. It combines components including social and labour standards, biodiversity, pesticide use, soil, and water use. The land use and zero deforestation aspect of the RTRS standard connects it to REDD+ carbon markets, opening additional financing for certification efforts.
