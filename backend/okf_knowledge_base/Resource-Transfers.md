# Resource Transfers
The provision of cash, vouchers, food, or other goods to vulnerable households. These transfers can be conditional or unconditional and are the primary mechanism of safety net programs. They serve to ensure basic consumption needs are met during crises or lean seasons, prevent the erosion of livelihood assets, and enable households to save and invest in more productive and resilient practices, which is a core function in supporting Climate-Smart Agriculture.
