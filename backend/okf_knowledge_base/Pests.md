# Pests
Pests are a significant challenge in agriculture, with climate change potentially perturbing the balance between pests and their predators. Inappropriate [[Insecticide]] use can disrupt natural predator populations, leading to pest outbreaks. Sustainable intensification emphasizes relying on natural processes of predation or biocontrol for pest problems. Plant breeding also aims to improve resistance to pests.
