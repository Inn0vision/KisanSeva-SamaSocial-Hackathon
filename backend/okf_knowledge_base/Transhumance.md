# Transhumance
A traditional agricultural practice involving the seasonal movement of livestock (such as cattle, sheep, or goats) between fixed summer and winter pastures. The text cites the Fulbe ethnic group in Northern Burkina Faso, for whom transhumance is a core component of cultural identity, proof of independence and self-worth for men, and is highly prized despite becoming increasingly difficult due to climate change and land cultivation.
