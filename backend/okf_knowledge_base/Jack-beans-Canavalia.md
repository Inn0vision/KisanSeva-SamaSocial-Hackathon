# Jack-beans (Canavalia)
A 'pioneer' crop known for its deep penetrating tap-roots, which are capable of breaking subsoil compactions. It can be incorporated into crop rotations or intercropped as a green manure cover crop to improve [[Soil (General)]] structure and health.
