# Crop
Crops are plants cultivated for food, feed, fiber, or other economic purposes. In the context of the agrifood system, crop production is a primary component, consuming energy for activities like irrigation, land preparation, and harvesting. The text highlights the importance of energy inputs per crop per hectare and discusses how increased energy input has led to greater productivity in cultivated areas. Crops are also a source of bioenergy, and their management practices, such as crop rotation and permanent crop cover, are crucial for energy efficiency and climate-smart agriculture.
