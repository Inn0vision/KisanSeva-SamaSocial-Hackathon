# Refrigerated Transport
A key technology within the cold chain that is vital for reducing post-harvest food losses, particularly for perishable goods. The text discusses its role in serving the global food industry and mentions ongoing research into reducing its energy consumption and environmental impacts. It is a critical component for maintaining food quality from production to market.
