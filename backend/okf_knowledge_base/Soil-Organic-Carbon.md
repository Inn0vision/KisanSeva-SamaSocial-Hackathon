# Soil Organic Carbon
Carbon stored in organic matter within the soil. Its accumulation and reduction of greenhouse gas emissions from [[Conservation Agriculture]] are highlighted. Increasing soil carbon storage is a mitigation benefit of reduced [[Grazing Management]] pressure and improved [[Pasture Management]]. It also contributes to [[Soil Health]].
