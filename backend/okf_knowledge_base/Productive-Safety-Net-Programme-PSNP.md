# Productive Safety Net Programme (PSNP)
A large-scale social protection program in Ethiopia that provides predictable cash and food support to chronically food-insecure households. A major component involves public works focused on community-based participatory watershed development, including activities like area enclosures, building terraces, woodlots, and irrigation systems. It is designed to protect assets, ensure basic food consumption, and build resilience against recurrent droughts, with documented benefits for environmental restoration and agricultural productivity. It is a prime example of a safety net program designed to support CSA principles.
