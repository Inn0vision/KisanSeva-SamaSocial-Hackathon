# Trade policy
Government policies that influence the international exchange of goods and services. In the fisheries and aquaculture sector, appropriately designed trade policies can contribute to eliminating economic distortions (e.g., harmful subsidies), enhance supply-side capacities, support adaptive capacities to climate change, and improve market access for products.
