# Cold Chain
A temperature-controlled supply chain that is essential for preserving the quality and safety of perishable food products from production to consumption. The text refers to it as a key challenge for improving health and the environment and notes that its performance is a significant factor in post-harvest losses. The integrity of the cold chain is a focus of climate-smart food systems.
