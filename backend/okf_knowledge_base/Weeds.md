# Weeds
Weeds are agricultural pests that compete with crops. Overuse of [[Herbicide]]s can lead to the emergence of herbicide-tolerant varieties of weeds, making their control more challenging. Sustainable intensification promotes biocontrol for weed problems.

# Weeds
Undesirable plants that grow in competition with cultivated crops. The text mentions that "hoeing or ploughing improves the seedbed and uproots weeds" and that conservation agriculture practices like "keeping the soil covered with a protective layer of mulch or crop residues... reduces weed growth." It also notes the use of "herbicides to manage weeds" in mechanized direct drill systems and "frequent weeding" as part of the System of Rice Intensification (SRI).
