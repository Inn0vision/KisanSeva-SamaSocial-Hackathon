# Pest (General)
Organisms that cause damage to crops, leading to unpredictability in agricultural production. Localized extreme events and sudden pest outbreaks are already increasing due to climate change. Increased outbreaks of new insect pests are projected with warming temperatures. [[Soil-borne pests]] are major limiting factors for crop production, especially on [[Degraded Soils]].

Effective management of pests, diseases, and weeds is a core component of Sustainable Crop Production Intensification (SCPI). [[Integrated Pest Management (IPM)]] and [[Integrated Weed Management (IWM)]] are crucial strategies for climate change adaptation, aiming to reduce negative impacts on crops and improve yields.
