# Integrated nutrient management
A CSA practice that involves the promotion of technologies to maintain soil fertility through the increased efficiency of fertilizers and organic fertilization. It is a top-ranking investment in Malawi's agricultural strategy, aimed at improving productivity and resource-use efficiency. It is part of a suite of practices that can generate mitigation co-benefits.
