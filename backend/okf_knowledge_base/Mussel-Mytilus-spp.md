# Mussel (Mytilus spp.)
A filter-feeding bivalve mollusk that is farmed with minimal or no greenhouse gas emissions as it does not require external feeds. Mussel farming can provide ecosystem services by extracting nutrients and reducing eutrophication risks. Common farmed species include *Mytilus galloprovincialis* in Spain and *Mytilus chilensis* in Chile.
