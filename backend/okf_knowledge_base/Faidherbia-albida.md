# Faidherbia albida
Faidherbia albida is a specific type of nitrogen-fixing leguminous tree mentioned for its ability to increase [[Soil Fertility]] and yields. Its development has been considerable in Niger through farmer-managed natural regeneration, supported by community-led projects and relaxed forestry measures. It is a key component in [[Agroforestry]] systems.
