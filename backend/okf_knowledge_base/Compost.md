# Compost
Organic matter that has been decomposed through a biooxidation process. Benefits include reducing soil erosion, increasing water retention, releasing nutrients for plant growth, and suppressing soil-borne pests and diseases. Using compost diverts organic waste from landfills, reducing methane emissions.
