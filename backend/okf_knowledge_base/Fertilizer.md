# Fertilizer
An agricultural input mentioned in the context of conservation farming, where its application is concentrated in permanent planting basins or rows to increase efficiency. The text also notes that policy processes can lead to modifications in fertilizer use, resulting in less waste and more efficient land use as an impact. Inefficient use is linked to GHG emissions.
