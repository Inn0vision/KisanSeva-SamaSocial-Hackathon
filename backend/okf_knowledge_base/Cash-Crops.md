# Cash Crops
Cash crops are agricultural products grown for sale to generate income. The text mentions that peatlands have been drained for the cultivation of cash crops, such as [[Palm Oil]] and other plantations, leading to significant [[Soil Degradation]] and GHG emissions.

# Cash Crops
Cash crops are crops grown for commercial sale. The text notes that the monoculture of cash crops, often combined with high use of external inputs, has been adopted by farmers to achieve high yields with minimal labor. However, this approach can lead to soil degradation and reliance on energy-intensive fertilizers and pesticides.
