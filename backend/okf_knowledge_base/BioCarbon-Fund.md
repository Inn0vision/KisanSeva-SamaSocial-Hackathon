# BioCarbon Fund
A public-private trust fund managed by the World Bank’s Carbon Finance Unit. It specifically provides carbon finance for projects that create GHG reduction in forest, agro, and other ecosystems. It is an unusual player for its active exploration of opportunities to create carbon credits from carbon sinks. It signed the first Emission Reduction Purchase Agreement for soil carbon credits with the Kenya Agricultural Carbon Project.
