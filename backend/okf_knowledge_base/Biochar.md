# Biochar
Biochar is a charcoal-like substance produced from biomass pyrolysis, which can be returned to the soil as an amendment. It is highlighted for its capacity to sequester carbon, with an estimated 65% of its carbon not oxidizing in the soil. Biochar production is part of integrated food-energy systems, such as the TOSOLY Farm, where it contributes to soil health and carbon sequestration.
