# Smallholder Farmers
Resource-poor farmers, herders, and fishing communities who are central to achieving climate-smart agriculture. The text emphasizes that these groups are particularly vulnerable to climate change and require institutional support to adopt new practices. This support includes access to knowledge, financial services, credit, markets, and mechanisms for collective action. Their successful transition to CSA is crucial for improving food security and requires overcoming significant resource constraints.
