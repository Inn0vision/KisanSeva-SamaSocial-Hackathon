# Fish
Fish resources are crucial for the livelihoods of communities in the Estero Real estuary. The ecosystem approach to fisheries and aquaculture (EAFA) aims to maintain and increase food and income from fish products while preserving ecosystem services. Unsustainable fishing practices, such as the use of fishing bags, collect fish larvae.
