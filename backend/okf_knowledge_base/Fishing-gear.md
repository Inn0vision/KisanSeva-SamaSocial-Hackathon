# Fishing gear
The equipment used to catch fish and other aquatic animals. It is categorized into passive gear (e.g., traps, lines, gill nets, longlines) and active gear (e.g., dredges, seines, trawls). The choice of gear significantly influences the fuel efficiency and environmental impact of fishing operations, with passive gear generally associated with lower impact and fuel efficiency (LIFE fishing). Climate change responses often involve adapting gear use, timing, and practices to changing conditions.
