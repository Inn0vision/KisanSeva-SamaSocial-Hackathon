# Conservation Farming
A set of sustainable land management techniques for cropping systems. The five basic techniques mentioned are: retaining crop residues; concentrating tillage and fertilizer application in a permanent grid; completing land preparation in the dry season; aggressive weeding; and intercropping or rotating nitrogen-fixing legumes on up to 30% of cultivated area. These practices are noted to bring significant benefits but face considerable adoption barriers.
