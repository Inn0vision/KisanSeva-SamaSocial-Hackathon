# Quinoa
Quinoa is mentioned as another native crop cultivated by farmers in the southern Peruvian Andes, alongside potatoes. It is part of the diverse agricultural systems that utilize traditional techniques like "raised fields" (waru-waru) to adapt to challenging environmental conditions.
