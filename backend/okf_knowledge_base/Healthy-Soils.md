# Healthy Soils
Soils that are rich in [[Soil Organic Matter]] and support a diverse community of [[Microorganisms]] and [[Invertebrates]]. These organisms contribute to essential ecosystem services, including the decomposition of organic matter and nitrogen fixation, which are vital for maintaining soil fertility and overall agricultural productivity. Healthy soils are fundamental for sustainable agriculture and food security.
