# Rhizobia
_Rhizobia_ are symbiotic bacteria that play a key role in [[Nitrogen Fixation]]. They associate with the roots of [[Legumes]], forming nodules where they convert atmospheric nitrogen into forms usable by plants (nitrate and ammonium ions). The text notes that nitrogen-fixing _Rhizobia_ generally do not thrive in [[Acidic Soils]], highlighting the importance of [[Soil pH]] for their activity and, consequently, for soil fertility.
