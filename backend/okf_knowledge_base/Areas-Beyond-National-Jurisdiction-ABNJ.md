# Areas Beyond National Jurisdiction (ABNJ)
The transboundary areas of the ocean that are not under the jurisdiction of any single state. The text highlights the political complexity of resource management in these areas and notes that the need for effective resource management in ABNJ will become increasingly important under climate change.
