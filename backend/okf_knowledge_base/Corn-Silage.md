# Corn Silage
Corn silage is a component of livestock feed. Studies on dairy farms in France showed that the efficiency of energy consumption per unit of output varied significantly, even within categories defined by the importance of corn silage in the feed system. This indicates potential for management improvements regardless of the specific feed composition.
