# Acidification
A biophysical change stemming from climate change, specifically referring to the acidification of aquatic environments. It is listed as a driver that interacts with pollutants and pathogens, posing potential risks to productivity, stocks, and human health. In aquaculture, it is identified as an impact area that can affect shell formation, with potential responses including adapting production techniques, moving production zones, and species selection.
