# Sugar Cane
Sugar cane is a versatile crop cultivated for food, livestock feed, and energy production. In integrated food-energy systems, its stalk can be fractionated into juice (for pigs and human consumption) and residual bagasse (used as fuel for gasifiers). The tops are used as basal diet for cattle and goats. Sugar cane is a key component in biorefineries that produce food, ethanol, heat, and electricity.
