# Agrosilvopastoral Systems
Integrated landscape-based systems that mix agriculture, trees, and livestock. These systems involve introducing trees and improved pasture into grazing lands. Benefits include: improved fodder and shade for animals, lower heat stress, reduced soil degradation, increased meat/milk yields, improved water infiltration, increased biodiversity, reduced methane emissions, and greater carbon capture. This approach aims for a 'triple win' of increased productivity, enhanced resilience, and reduced emissions.
