# Lupine
A 'pioneer' crop characterized by its deep penetrating tap-roots. These roots can effectively break subsoil compactions, improving soil structure. Lupine can be planted in crop rotation or intercropped as a green manure cover crop to enhance [[Soil (General)]] health.
