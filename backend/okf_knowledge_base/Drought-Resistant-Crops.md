# Drought-Resistant Crops
Breeding for resistance to droughts and floods is an investment option for climate change adaptation. The use of crops or varieties with better resilience to dry spells is preferred. Examples include specific [[Wheat]] and [[Beans]] varieties. However, the text notes that there is little prospect for breakthroughs in developing truly "drought resistant" crops in the near future.
