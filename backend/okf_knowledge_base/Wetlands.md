# Wetlands
Wetlands are critical ecosystems, often encompassing peatlands and mires. They play a role in the water cycle and are impacted by land use changes and climate variability. Examples include the Zoige wetlands on the Eastern Tibetan Plateau, where rangeland privatization has had impacts.
