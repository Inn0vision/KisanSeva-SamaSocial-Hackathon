# Ukraine
A major global producer and exporter of wheat. The text discusses its implementation of an export ban during the 2010 drought, which damaged its reputation as a reliable supplier. This led to a policy reconsideration, initiating a targeted social protection system to ensure low-income people can buy food during high prices while maintaining farmer incentives.
