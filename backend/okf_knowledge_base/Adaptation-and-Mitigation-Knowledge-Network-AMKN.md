# Adaptation and Mitigation Knowledge Network (AMKN)
A map-based online platform created by CCAFS that aggregates climate, agricultural, and socioeconomic information. It features photos and videos of farmers and displays data such as crop adaptations to climate change, drought indexes, and global climate models. It serves as an information service for practitioners, donors, policymakers, and researchers interested in food security and climate change.
