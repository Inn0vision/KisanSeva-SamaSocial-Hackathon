# Soil Fixation
Soil fixation refers to the process of stabilizing soil, often through the use of vegetation, to prevent degradation. In the context of participatory rangeland management in the Syrian Arab Republic, reintroducing native plants is described as helping to "fix the soil" and stop [[Sand Encroachment]], contributing to the restoration of degraded [[Rangelands]].
