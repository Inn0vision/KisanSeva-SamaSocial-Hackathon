# Nitrogen
Nitrogen is a crucial [[Soil Nutrients]] that plays a key role in plant metabolism and growth. Its lack often limits plant growth, as plants cannot directly use gaseous nitrogen from the atmosphere. However, atmospheric nitrogen can be converted into nitrate and ammonium ions in the soil through [[Nitrogen Fixation]] by certain soil microorganisms, such as symbiotic _Rhizobia_ bacteria associated with [[Legumes]] and non-symbiotic bacteria like _Clostridium_ and _Azoterbacter_. Nitrogen also deserves special attention because it can be a cause of pollution when it leaches as nitrates into the water table, and it can be released from agro-ecosystems as nitrous oxide (N2O), a serious greenhouse gas affecting global warming.
