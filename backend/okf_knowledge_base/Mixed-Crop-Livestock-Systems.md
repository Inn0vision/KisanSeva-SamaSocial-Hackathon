# Mixed Crop-Livestock Systems
Agricultural systems that integrate crop production with livestock raising, often involving rotating pastures with crops to supply additional nutrients. Supported as a Climate-Smart Agriculture approach in Brazil, these systems enhance sustainability and resource efficiency.
