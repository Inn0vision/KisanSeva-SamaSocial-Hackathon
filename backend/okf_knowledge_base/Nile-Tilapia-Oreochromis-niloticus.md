# Nile Tilapia (Oreochromis niloticus)
The Nile tilapia (*Oreochromis niloticus*) is identified as one of the two principal species used in West African fish culture and is native to the Volta River Basin. The Tilapia Volta Project focuses on protecting native gene pools of tilapia by characterizing wild genetic resources and establishing conservation plans, while also developing improved aquaculture strains with better production characteristics (e.g., improved growth rates) for climate change adaptation.
