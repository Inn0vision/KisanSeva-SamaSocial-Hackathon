# Tomatoes
Tomatoes are a perishable food crop. A study compared the carbon dioxide equivalents of tomatoes grown in greenhouses in Denmark, the Netherlands, and Sweden versus those grown under the sun in Spain, finding the latter had a lower environmental impact.
