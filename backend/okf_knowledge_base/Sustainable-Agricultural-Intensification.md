# Sustainable Agricultural Intensification
A strategy for increasing agricultural output from the same area of land while reducing negative environmental impacts and enhancing natural capital and the flow of ecosystem services. It is a foundational principle for Climate-Smart Agriculture (CSA) and is built upon activities that increase resource use efficiency. It involves a range of possible options, including maintaining healthy soil, using well-adapted high-yielding varieties, integrating pest management, and efficient water management.
