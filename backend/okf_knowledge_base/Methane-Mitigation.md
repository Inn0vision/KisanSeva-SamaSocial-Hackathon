# Methane Mitigation
Activities aimed at reducing methane emissions. A notable exception in carbon markets is methane avoidance through manure management, which has an approved Clean Development Mechanism methodology. It is also a focus area under the GEF-6 strategy for financing Climate-Smart Agriculture activities.
