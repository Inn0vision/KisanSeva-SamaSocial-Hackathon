# Climate and Development Knowledge Network
A network that supports decision-makers in designing and delivering climate-compatible development by combining research, advisory services, and knowledge management. It works at national, regional, and global levels in partnership with public, private, and non-governmental sectors. It is led by PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP and managed by an alliance of organizations.
