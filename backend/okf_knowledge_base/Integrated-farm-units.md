# Integrated farm units
Usually inland ponds or paddies integrated with plants and animals; encompassing a range of options. This system faces marginal shifts in productivity and disease risks in some or all of its components due to climate change. Response strategies include adjusting the integration mix, possibly increasing its use, improving management, and monitoring environmental conditions and early warning systems.
