# Pulses
An important category of local food crops, especially in developing countries, serving as a key source of protein. In farming systems, pulses contribute to input-saving and resource-conserving technologies because they fix nitrogen in the soil, thereby reducing the need for [[Chemical Fertilizers]]. Their cultivation also helps to reduce [[Soil Pathogens]].
