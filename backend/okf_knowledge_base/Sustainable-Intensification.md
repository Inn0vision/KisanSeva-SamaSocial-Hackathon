# Sustainable Intensification
Policies and practices for intensifying sustainable production and enhancing productivity vital for Climate-Smart Agriculture development. These include improved technologies and innovations; efficient use of land, water, energy and other inputs; improved access to information and infrastructure; and efficient markets and risk management tools. Must be combined with broader landscape restoration measures.
