# REDD+
Stands for Reducing Emissions from Deforestation and Forest Degradation. A mechanism aimed at creating financial value for the carbon stored in forests, offering incentives for developing countries to reduce emissions from forested lands and invest in low-carbon paths to sustainable development. UNFCCC negotiations on REDD+ are difficult and slow, with intense disagreements on issues related to verification and financing. It is considered a signal for the potential inclusion of agricultural carbon sequestration in future global emission agreements. A significant amount of readiness activities have been ongoing for REDD+.
