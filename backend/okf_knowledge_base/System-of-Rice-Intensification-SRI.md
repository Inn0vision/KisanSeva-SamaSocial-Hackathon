# System of Rice Intensification (SRI)
An approach to rice cultivation that significantly increases rice yields while simultaneously reducing requirements for seeds, water, and chemical inputs. Originating in Madagascar, it has been successfully tested in over 30 countries across West Africa, Latin America, and Asia. It addresses the special challenges of GHG emissions from paddy rice production systems.
