# Leguminous Species
Plants belonging to the legume family, known for their ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen. The text mentions "use leguminous species to replenish soil nutrients" in conservation agriculture and that they "can fix nitrogen (N) through symbiotic Rhizobium." They are also noted for their lower C/N ratio and fast breakdown.
