# Fertilizer Management
The practice of managing the application of nutrients to soil to improve crop yields while minimizing environmental impact. It is listed as an example of an activity that could receive financing under the GEF-6 strategy for Climate-Smart Agriculture. Carbon accounting methodologies for nutrient management have also been developed under voluntary carbon market standards.
