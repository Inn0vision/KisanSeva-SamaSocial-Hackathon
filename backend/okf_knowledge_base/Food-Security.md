# Food Security
A state where all people, at all times, have physical, social and economic access to sufficient, safe and nutritious food that meets their dietary needs and food preferences for an active and healthy life. The text frames the reduction of post-harvest losses and the development of climate-smart agriculture as fundamental strategies for achieving improved food security, especially in the context of a changing climate.
