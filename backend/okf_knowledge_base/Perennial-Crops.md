# Perennial Crops
Crops that live for more than two years, often producing harvests over multiple seasons. The text states that "Increasing the use of perennial crops and maintenance of shrubs and trees in the farm landscape improves soil resilience" and that "Perennial crops and trees can sequester substantial amounts of C and can store C for longer periods than annuals."
