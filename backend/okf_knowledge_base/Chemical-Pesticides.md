# Chemical Pesticides
Synthetic substances used to control pests in agricultural systems. The text highlights that their use is minimized or avoided in sustainable agricultural practices, such as [[Rice-fish agriculture systems]], to support the conservation of agricultural biodiversity and reduce environmental impact.
