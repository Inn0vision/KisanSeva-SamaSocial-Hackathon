# Trawls
A type of active fishing gear used in both small-scale and commercial fishing. In small-scale fishing, it is used for species that are a key source of higher-value market supply. In commercial fishing, it targets important demersal and shellfish species. Trawl fisheries, especially those with poor catches, can be among the most GHG-intensive global food systems due to high fuel use. Climate responses include adapting gear and practices, and improving stock monitoring.
