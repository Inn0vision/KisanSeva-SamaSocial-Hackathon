# Aquaculture
The cultivation of aquatic organisms, including fish, molluscs, and plants. It encompasses a wide range of systems, from extensive traditional methods to highly intensive, controlled operations. Key systems include seabed mollusc culture, suspended mollusc culture, various pond systems (extensive, partially fed, completely fed, integrated), intensive tank and raceway systems, cage systems, and integrated multi-trophic aquaculture (IMTA). Aquaculture is significantly affected by climate change through disruptions in productivity, water quality, temperature, and disease risks. Response strategies are diverse and system-specific, including adjusting locations, management practices, species mix, stock monitoring, and improving system resilience.
