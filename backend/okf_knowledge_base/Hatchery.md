# Hatchery
A facility for the artificial breeding and hatching of fish or shellfish eggs, crucial for providing reliable seed production for aquaculture systems. Hatcheries are often part of intensive tank and raceway systems and are vital for ensuring stock viability in the face of climate disruptions. They are highlighted as a key response to ensure reliable seed production for systems like suspended mollusc culture and completely fed pond systems, helping to adapt to changing environmental conditions.
