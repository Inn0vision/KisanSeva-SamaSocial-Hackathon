# Sustainable agricultural intensification
An agricultural development model that forms an important basis for building CSA strategies. It focuses on producing more output from the same area of land while conserving resources, reducing negative environmental impacts, and enhancing natural capital. It is a response to major drivers of change like evolving food systems, increasing population-land ratios, and resource scarcity.
