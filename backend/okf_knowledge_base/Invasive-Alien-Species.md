# Invasive Alien Species
Non-native species that can cause significant harm to ecosystems, economies, or human health. Climate change is expected to create increasingly favorable conditions for their proliferation, posing risks to genetic resources and altering ecosystem dynamics. Their spread can exacerbate threats to native biodiversity and agricultural systems.
