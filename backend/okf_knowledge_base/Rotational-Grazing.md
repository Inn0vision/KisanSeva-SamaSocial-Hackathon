# Rotational Grazing
A pasture and grazing management practice that involves moving livestock between pastures to allow forage to recover. It can lead to higher livestock yields due to greater forage availability and quality. Short-term losses are likely if the system supports fewer head of livestock, but it can reduce variability in the long term where forage availability is a key factor.
