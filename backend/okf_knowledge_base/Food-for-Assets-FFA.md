# Food-for-Assets (FFA)
A type of public works safety net program where participants receive food or cash assistance in return for work on community asset creation or rehabilitation projects. These projects often focus on environmental conservation and improving agricultural infrastructure, such as building soil and water conservation structures, terraces, and irrigation systems. The created assets are an investment in future resilience and productivity, directly supporting CSA objectives.
