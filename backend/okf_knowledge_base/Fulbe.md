# Fulbe
An ethnic group in Northern Burkina Faso, specifically in the village of Biidi 2. Their cultural identity is deeply tied to transhumance (seasonal livestock herding), which they value over sedentary crop cultivation. This cultural preference, along with a history of dominance over the Rimaiibe, a reluctance to engage in wage labour or migration, and strongly defined gender roles, creates barriers to adopting new climate change adaptation strategies, leading to increased poverty and food insecurity.
