# Clean Development Mechanism
A mechanism defined under the Kyoto Protocol that allows emission-reduction projects in developing countries to earn certified emission reduction credits, which can be traded and used by industrialized countries to meet a part of their emission reduction targets. A limited set of afforestation and reforestation activities are already eligible for the CDM, albeit with temporary credits of limited fungibility.
