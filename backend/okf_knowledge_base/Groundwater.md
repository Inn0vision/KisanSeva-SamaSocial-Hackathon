# Groundwater
Groundwater refers to the water held underground in [[Aquifers]] in soil and rock crevices. It is a critical water resource, especially for irrigation, as highlighted by "Groundwater use for irrigation – a global inventory" and "Climate change and groundwater: India’s opportunities for mitigation and adaptation." Climate change can impact groundwater through changes in aquifer recharge. Groundwater is also susceptible to salinity, with a significant percentage of global groundwater being saline, affecting irrigated areas worldwide. Better groundwater management, including controlled pumping and salinity control, is an adaptive option.
