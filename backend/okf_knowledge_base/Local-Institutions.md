# Local Institutions
Defined broadly as the formal and informal 'rules of the game' that govern behavior, including organizations, social norms, and cultural conventions. The text identifies them as crucial for facilitating the adoption of climate-smart agriculture by smallholders. They support CSA by: 1) producing and sharing technical knowledge; 2) providing financial services and market access; and 3) supporting the coordination of collaborative action. Examples include farmer cooperatives, water user groups, savings and loans groups, and extension services.
