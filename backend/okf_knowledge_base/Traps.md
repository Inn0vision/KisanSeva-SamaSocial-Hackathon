# Traps
A type of passive fishing gear used in small-scale, artisanal, and casual fishing operations, often in inland or coastal subsistence fishing. This gear is used in systems vulnerable to changing species mixes and ecosystem disruptions. Response strategies to climate change include adapting to catch different species and using different locations.
