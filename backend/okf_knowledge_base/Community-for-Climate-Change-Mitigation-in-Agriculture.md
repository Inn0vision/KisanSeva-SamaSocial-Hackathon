# Community for Climate Change Mitigation in Agriculture
An online network launched by FAO in 2012 for practitioners working on reducing greenhouse gas emissions in agriculture. It offers a resource library and hosts discussions in member-only and public forums. The community had over 600 members and has delivered online learning programmes, such as one on agroforestry for over 250 participants.
