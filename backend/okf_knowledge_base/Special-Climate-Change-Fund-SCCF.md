# Special Climate Change Fund (SCCF)
A fund managed by the GEF under the UNFCCC with a broad mandate covering adaptation and mitigation. In practice, it has developed into a funding source for climate change adaptation activities accessible to non-least developed countries. Agriculture, land management, and water management are main target sectors, with more than half of its resources dispersed to projects in these areas. A significant challenge is the unpredictability of finance availability, as it relies on voluntary ad hoc contributions from donor countries.
