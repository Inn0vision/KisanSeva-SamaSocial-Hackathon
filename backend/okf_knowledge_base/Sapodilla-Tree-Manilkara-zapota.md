# Sapodilla Tree (Manilkara zapota)
The Sapodilla tree, also known as _Manilkara zapota_, was historically a primary source of latex (the raw material for chewing gum, also known as [[Chicle Gum]]) extraction in Guatemala's Petén state, particularly within the area now known as the Maya Biosphere Reserve. It is also a source of timber.
