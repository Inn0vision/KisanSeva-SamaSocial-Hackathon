# Crops (General)
Crops are central to agricultural production and food security. They are significantly impacted by climate change through increased evapotranspiration, changes in rainfall patterns, higher temperatures, and extreme weather events, leading to potential crop failures. The resilience of crops and agricultural biodiversity are key for sustainable management. Both rainfed and irrigated agriculture systems are affected.
