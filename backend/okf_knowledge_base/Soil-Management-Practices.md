# Soil Management Practices
Soil management practices encompass various techniques aimed at maintaining and improving soil health, quality, and productivity. Key practices mentioned include reduced or zero tillage, permanent crop cover, crop rotation, and the return of agricultural residues or manure to the soil. These practices are crucial for sequestering carbon, increasing soil organic matter, reducing soil erosion, and enhancing resilience to extreme weather events.
