# Trees and Shrubs (Agroforestry)
Trees and shrubs are key components of "Agroforestry" systems, which involve their use as part of agricultural systems. They contribute to preventing [[Soil Erosion]], facilitating [[Soil Water Retention]], diminishing impacts of extreme weather, diversifying income, and providing energy and fodder for livestock. These systems are promoted as a substitute for traditional slash-and-burn practices, increasing land and labor productivity and protecting the soil.
