# Disease (General)
Pathological conditions affecting crops, leading to unpredictability in agricultural production. Localized extreme events and sudden disease outbreaks are already increasing due to climate change. Increased outbreaks of new pathogens are projected with climate change. [[Soil-borne diseases]] are major limiting factors for crop production, especially on [[Degraded Soils]].

Effective management of pests, diseases, and weeds is a core component of Sustainable Crop Production Intensification (SCPI). [[Integrated Pest Management (IPM)]] (which often includes disease management) is a crucial strategy for climate change adaptation, aiming to reduce negative impacts on crops and improve yields. The System of Rice Intensification (SRI) has also been observed to result in no insect or disease infestation in fields.
