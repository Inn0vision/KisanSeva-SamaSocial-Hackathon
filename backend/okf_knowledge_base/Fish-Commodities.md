# Fish Commodities
Refers to products from capture fisheries and aquaculture. Major causes of loss include bycatch and discards during fishing, poor post-harvest handling, lack of storage and chilling facilities, and inadequate preservation and processing techniques (e.g., canning, smoking). Good practices involve improved bycatch management, better handling after landing, and investment in cooling facilities.
