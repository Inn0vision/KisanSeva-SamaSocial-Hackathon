# Phytoplankton
Microscopic aquatic plants that serve as the primary food source for filter-feeding bivalves like mussels. Mussel growth is dependent on the availability of phytoplankton in the water. Production is fueled by nutrients and can be exceptionally high in areas with upwelling systems, such as the Ria Arousa in Galicia, which contributes to high aquaculture yields.
