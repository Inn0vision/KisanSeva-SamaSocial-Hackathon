# Temperate Humid Grasslands (Soil context)
Temperate humid grasslands are agricultural systems found in temperate, humid climates. The text notes that these grasslands can lose about 30 percent of their [[Soil Organic Carbon (SOC)]] after 60 years of cultivation, highlighting the impact of land conversion on carbon stocks in these soil types.
