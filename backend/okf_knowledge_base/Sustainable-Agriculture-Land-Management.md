# Sustainable Agriculture Land Management
A set of practices that increase productivity and enhance resilience to climate change while sequestering carbon in the soil. It is the basis for generating carbon credits in voluntary markets, as demonstrated by the Kenya Agricultural Carbon Project. Quantification of carbon sequestration is monitored based on specific carbon accounting methodologies.
