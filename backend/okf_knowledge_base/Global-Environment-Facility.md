# Global Environment Facility
A multilateral financial mechanism that provides grants for projects related to biodiversity, climate change, international waters, land degradation, the ozone layer, and persistent organic pollutants. The ongoing process of the GEF-6 replenishment is starting to put more explicit emphasis on Climate-Smart Agriculture activities. It is a source of thematic financing that can be combined with other channels to support integrative approaches.
