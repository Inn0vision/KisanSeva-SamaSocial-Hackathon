# Crop Failures
Increased frequency of crop failures is expected as a result of changes in rainfall patterns and temperatures. These changes lead to greater variations in [[Soil Moisture]], particularly affecting [[Rainfed Farming]] in marginal areas. Extreme events like intense rainfalls, floods, and erosion further contribute to crop failures.
