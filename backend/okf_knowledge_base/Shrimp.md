# Shrimp
Shrimp are a key resource in the Estero Real estuary in Nicaragua, supporting both shrimp fisheries and aquaculture. Large-scale shrimp farming has increased, generating livelihoods but also creating social conflicts and environmental concerns, including the collection of small shrimp and fish larvae by unsustainable fishing practices.
