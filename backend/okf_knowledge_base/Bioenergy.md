# Bioenergy
Energy derived from biomass sources including woody biomass, crop residues, and dedicated energy crops. When produced sustainably, bioenergy presents an opportunity to address climate change by reducing carbon dioxide emissions. However, climate-smartness depends on the ecosystem, enabling environment, and alternative land use options.
