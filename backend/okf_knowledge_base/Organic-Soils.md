# Organic Soils
Organic soils are soils that have a substantial layer of organic matter near the surface, such as [[Peatlands]]. They are significant carbon sinks, containing 30 percent of the world's soil carbon despite covering only three percent of the global land area. Managing these soils is crucial due to their significant emission potential when drained.
