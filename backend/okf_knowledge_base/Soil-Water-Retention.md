# Soil Water Retention
Soil water retention refers to the ability of soil to hold moisture, which is crucial for plant growth and resilience. Agroforestry systems lead to less variable yields partly due to better humidity retention in the soils. Increasing [[Soil Organic Matter]] and combining it with added soil cover increases water retention, making agricultural systems more resilient to variability of precipitation and extreme events.
