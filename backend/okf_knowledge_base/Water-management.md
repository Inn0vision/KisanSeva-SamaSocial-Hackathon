# Water management
A critical CSA investment category for adaptation, particularly in drylands. Practices include rehabilitating and developing irrigation schemes, establishing rainwater harvesting systems (dams, box ridges), and strengthening technical capacity for irrigation. It is a practice with high up-front investment costs that can show a positive marginal abatement cost, implying a need for external finance.
