# Chorkor oven
A fuel-efficient fish smoking oven introduced to improve post-harvest operations in small-scale fisheries. It is well-known for its fuel efficiency performance, reducing by two-thirds the ratio of wood used to fish produced compared with traditional ovens. It also produces less smoke and results in higher quality smoked fish products. This technical intervention, combined with organizational and entrepreneurial skills development, has been shown to increase the incomes and self-esteem of beneficiaries, allowing them to compete in more lucrative markets.
