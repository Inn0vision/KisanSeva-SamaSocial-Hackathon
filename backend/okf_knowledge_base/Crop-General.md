# Crop (General)
A general term encompassing cultivated plants, which are central to agricultural production, global food security, and the livelihoods of rural communities. Crop productivity is highly sensitive to temperature changes, with potential increases in colder environments and decreases in warmer ones. Climate change is projected to significantly impact crop production, leading to increased unpredictability due to extreme events and outbreaks of pests and diseases.

Sustainable Crop Production Intensification (SCPI) aims to improve crop performance under changing conditions by focusing on practices like cultivating a wider range of species and varieties, using quality seeds, and adopting integrated management approaches. Crop diversification, alterations in cropping patterns and rotations, and the use of high-quality, adapted varieties are key strategies for climate change adaptation and mitigation.
