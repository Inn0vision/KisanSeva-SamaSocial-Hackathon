# National Adaptation Programme of Action
A process for Least Developed Countries to identify priority activities that respond to their urgent and immediate needs to adapt to climate change. These assessments, along with Nationally Appropriate Mitigation Actions, provide a solid basis for concrete and country-specific preparatory measures for Climate-Smart Agriculture. They are part of the existing knowledge and experiences that form a basis for readiness activities.
